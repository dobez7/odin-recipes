Pasta Pesto and Cheese

Description

A childhood favourite of mine. An extremely simple recipe, consisting of pasta, pesto and cheese. Chicken and tuna can also be mixed in.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • pesto
  • cheese
  • tuna

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Mix in the pesto and tuna.
  3. Grate on the cheese.
  4. Bosh.
